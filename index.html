Before you settle in
Good to Know
🔌 UK plug sockets
Malta uses three-pin UK-style plugs. Bring a universal adapter if needed.
🚿 Hot water
Boiler has limited capacity. Allow 10–15 min after a long shower.
👟 Comfortable shoes
Valletta has steep streets and cobblestones. Good footwear makes a real difference.
🚶 Walkable city
Most things you'll want to see are within a 10–15 minute walk.
🗣️ Language
English is widely spoken. A few words of Maltese go a long way.
💳 Currency
Malta uses the Euro (€). Cards are widely accepted.
☕ Coffee machine
The apartment has a Dolce Gusto machine.
🧴 Toiletries
Shampoo and body wash are provided. Just bring anything else you need.
